Things to do before, during and after a Typhoon

Before the typhoon it is highly recommended to prepare an emergency bag containing necessary things. As it will not be easy to navigate these materials during the typhoon and it will be very essential after the typhoon because most of the basic needs will be damaged and all tap waters are not safe along with that performing activities like cooking is risky during the typhoon. Also create a plan for the members of the family to follow and if the house is not stable evacuate as soon as possible.

prepare an emergency bag

An emergency bag should contain the following:

Clean bottled water
Canned goods
Flashlight
Battery powered radios
First Aid kit
Maintenance & Medicine
secure house
If you have pets save enough food and water
Make a safety plan for your pets<
Evacuate you house if needed

During the typhoon

Stay tune/updated from the news
Make sure every member is close by
Don't panick, relax although it is normal to stress when witnessing traumatizing situations

After the typhoon
Stay tuned on the radio
identify damages in your house
Avoid infrasturuce that may erode anytime
If the typhoon has passed you can start cleaning your area
If there are water in random objects it should be removed to prevent moquitos
Check if the whole family is present
if evacuated wait for the instructions to asure if its safe to return home
